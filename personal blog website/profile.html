Helping Hands-Rgukt Nuzvid

A Students's Initiative- An Abode of Service

(Regd No. 313/2019 under the A.P. Societies Registration Act., 2001)


Student of RGUKT Nuzvid initiated their journey of service in 2009 lead them to establish a foundation,Helping Hands.
It mainly focueses on the well-being of the students from the poor background on the campus as well as in society. Helping Hands is a student-volunteer organization committed for the welfare of student community in RGUKT-Nuzvid, a A.P state government university which caters to the educational needs of rural youth of Andhra Pradesh.
